Website Project Plan – Mma Cake Bliss

Site Name

Name: Mma Cake Bliss

Reason: “Mma Cake Bliss” was chosen because it reflects the joy and indulgence people associate with delicious, beautifully crafted cakes. The name is simple, memorable, and appropriate for branding a bakery or cake business.

Optional Domain: mmacakeblissbakes.com (availability can be checked via a domain registrar)

Site Purpose

The website aims to present a visually delightful and easy-to-navigate platform for a cake business. It will provide information about the company, showcase featured cakes, enable visitors to contact the business, and explore cake products with the intention to support online shopping in the future.

Scenarios

  • What types of cakes does Mma Cake Bliss offer and how do I place an order?
  • How can I contact Cake Bliss to ask about custom cake designs?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color – Soft Pink (#f9c6d1): Used for backgrounds, section highlights, buttons, and accents.
  • Secondary Color – Chocolate Brown (#6b3e26): Used for headings, icons, and footer background.

Typography

  • Headings – Playfair Display (serif): Elegant and stylish, perfect for titles and product names.
  • Body Text – Poppins (sans-serif): Clean and readable for all paragraphs, forms, and product descriptions.

Project Reason

I chose this subject because I enjoy working with visually appealing content, and a cake website offers an opportunity to design something colorful, creative, and engaging. It also allows me to explore basic e-commerce elements and user interaction features that are valuable in real-world web development.
