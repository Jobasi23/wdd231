Urban-Day Chamber of Commerce

The Urban-Day Chamber of Commerce is dedicated to supporting local businesses,
fostering economic growth, and building a vibrant community.
We provide resources, networking opportunities,
and promote local events to help businesses thrive in Urban-Day.

Advocate for local businesses at the city and state level.

Host regular workshops, seminars, and training sessions for business owners.

Facilitate networking events to connect entrepreneurs and professionals.

Maintain a comprehensive business directory to promote local services.

Support community initiatives and encourage local shopping.
